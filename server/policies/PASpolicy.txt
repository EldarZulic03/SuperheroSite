Privacy and Security Policy

Last Updated on December 6, 2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to Superhero Site!. This Privacy and Security Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our web application.

2. Information We Collect

- Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email, password, and nickname. This information is used for authentication and account management.

- Profile Information: Users can choose to provide additional information, such as a profile picture and a brief bio, to personalize their accounts.

- Search Data: We collect information related to hero searches, including search terms and results, to improve the search functionality.

- Lists and Reviews: If you create hero lists or add reviews, we store the associated information, including list names, hero details, ratings, and comments.

3. How We Use Your Information

- Authentication: Your account information is used for secure login and authentication.

- Personalization: We use your profile information to personalize your experience on our platform.

- Improvement of Services: Search and usage data help us enhance the functionality and performance of our application.

- Communication: We may contact you regarding important updates or to respond to your inquiries.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. This includes encryption, regular security assessments, and access controls.

5. Information Sharing

- Public Lists and Reviews:* If you mark your hero lists as public, they will be visible to other users, including list names, hero details, and reviews.

- Administrators: Our administrators may access user information for site maintenance and management.

- Legal Compliance: We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights and the rights of others.

6. Your Choices

- Account Settings: You can manage your account settings, including privacy preferences and notifications.

- Deactivation: If you choose to deactivate your account, your information will be retained for a limited time for legal and operational purposes.

7. Administrator Functions

Our administrators have access to user accounts for site maintenance, copyright enforcement, and resolving disputes. This includes the ability to disable accounts or hide content that violates our policies.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for authentication, analytics, and improving user experience. You can manage your cookie preferences in your browser settings.

9. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy and Security Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the platform or via email.

If you have questions or concerns about this policy, please contact us at admin@admin.com
