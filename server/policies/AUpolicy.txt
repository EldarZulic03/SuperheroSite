Last Updated on December 6, 2023

Welcome to Superhero Site! This Acceptable Use Policy outlines the acceptable and prohibited uses of our web application. By using our application, you agree to comply with this policy.

You are expressly prohibited from engaging in the following activities on or through our web application:

- Unauthorized Access: Attempting to access accounts, systems, or data belonging to other users without permission.

- Malicious Use: Using our platform for any malicious or harmful activities, including but not limited to hacking, phishing, or distributing malware.

- Abuse of Resources: Engaging in activities that may overload, harm, or impair the functionality of our application, including the use of automated tools without our consent.

- Illegal Content: Uploading, sharing, or promoting any content that violates applicable laws, including but not limited to copyright infringement, hate speech, or illegal activities.

- False Information: Providing false, misleading, or inaccurate information when creating an account, submitting reviews, or engaging in any other activity on our platform.

- Harassment: Engaging in any form of harassment, bullying, or threatening behavior towards other users or administrators.

- Violation of Privacy: Unauthorized attempts to access or collect private information from other users.

2. User Responsibilities

- Compliance with Laws: Users are expected to comply with all applicable laws and regulations while using our application.

- Account Security: Users are responsible for maintaining the security of their accounts, including passwords and access credentials.

- Respectful Conduct: Users are expected to treat others with respect and courtesy while using our platform.

3. Reporting Violations

If you become aware of any violation of this Acceptable Use Policy or suspect unauthorized or inappropriate activities, please report it to admin@admin.com.

4. Consequences of Violation

Violations of this policy may result in actions such as account suspension, content removal, or legal action, depending on the severity and nature of the violation.

5. Policy Changes

We may update this Acceptable Use Policy periodically. Users will be notified of significant changes through the platform or via email.
